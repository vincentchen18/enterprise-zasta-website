Our Story

Built on craft,
grown on trust.

Zasta Embroidery and Uniform is an Australian-registered private company, established in 1999, for embroidery services. At that time, it was only providing digitising and embroidery services.

In 2018, Zasta was purchased by its new owner Jacky, who brought a commitment to delivering better quality and faster services to the community.

Zasta is now a one-stop uniform company, supplying clothing, caps, bags, and promotional products, as well as digitising, embroidery, screen printing, and heat transfer. From an idea to reality, every step of the process is arranged by us.

No order is too complex or too small. We are dedicated to maximising value for your money and are ready to assist with products suited to your individual or business needs. Let Zasta be your best mate!
